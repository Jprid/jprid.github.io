John Pridmore is a Full-Stack Software Engineer working at Relativity (formerly known as kCura) in Chicago, IL.

At Relativity he works on their in-house Document Processing Engine -- Invariant.

During his final year at DePaul University, he served as the interim Vice President of its Computer Science Society, and helped plan the inaugural DemonHacks (DePaul's Hackathon).

Additionally, during his undergraduate studies, he studied abroad at Ritsumeikan University in Kyoto, Japan. There he studied Japanese at a N2 level for 10 months. He remains conversational in Japanese.
